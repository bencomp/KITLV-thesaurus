Archipelago Principle

Scope note:

The principle that islands, waters and other natural features of an archipelago are to be regarded as a single geographical, economic and political unit (codified as the UN Convention on the Law of the Sea, 1982).

Broader Terms:

maritime law

Related Terms:

territorial waters
