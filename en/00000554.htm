militia

Scope note:

A part of the organized armed forces of a country liable to be called up in emergency or the whole body of able-bodied citizens declared by law as to be subject to be called up for military service.

Broader Terms:

armed forces

Narrower Terms:

civic guard

Related Terms:

civil defence
