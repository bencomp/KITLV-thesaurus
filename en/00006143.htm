nuclear installations

Scope note:

Any reactor and any other facility in which substantial amount of nuclear substances, nuclear fuels or radioisotopes are manufactured, processed, handled, used or stored; excludes nuclear propulsed vehicles.

Broader Terms:

industrial plants

Related Terms:

nuclear energy
