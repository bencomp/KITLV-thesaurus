people's economy

Scope note:

Economic system focusing primarily on promoting the essential needs and welfare of the people (esp. the lower socio-economic strata) rather than maximizing profit, and managed democratically, giving precedence to labour over capital.

Broader Terms:

economic systems

Translation:

volkseconomie /ekonomi kerakyatan /démocratie économique /economía de democracia
