shamanism

Scope note:

Refers to a range of traditional beliefs and practices similar to Animism that claim the ability to diagnose and cure human suffering and, in some societies, the ability to cause suffering. This is believed to be accomplished by forming a special relationship with, or gaining control over, spirits.
